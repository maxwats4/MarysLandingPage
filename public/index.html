about mary watson

An artist that can do anything you need

I've been an artist for as long as I can remember, but I started pursuing it seriously at 17. For the past eight years, I've focused primarily on commissioned watercolor paintings, though I enjoy working across a variety of mediums. Watercolor and digital art are my favorites, each offering its own kind of magic.

Lately, I've been leaning more into digital work and exploring the world of book cover design. My goal is to turn that passion into a full-time thing. There is nothing more satisfying than seeing people appreciate and love what I do.
