About Mary Watson

A passionate artist exploring the beauty of life through various mediums

Mary Watson is a contemporary artist based in Helena, Montana, whose work spans across multiple mediums including oil paintings, watercolors, charcoal drawings, and mixed media installations. With over 15 years of experience, Mary's artistic journey has been one of constant exploration and growth.

Her work draws inspiration from the natural landscapes of Montana, human emotions, and the interplay between light and shadow. Each piece is carefully crafted to evoke a sense of wonder and connection with the viewer.
